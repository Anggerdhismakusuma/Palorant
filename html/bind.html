MAP - BIND

Bind is a compact, two-site map known for its teleporter system, which allows for fast rotations and unpredictable plays. Unlike other maps, Bind lacks a traditional Mid area, meaning teams must rely on site control and strategic flanks rather than mid dominance.

With two bomb sites (A and B), Bind offers a mix of tight chokepoints, short-range engagements, and open spaces, making it ideal for aggressive playstyles and quick executes. The teleporters, located near A Short and B Long, let players reposition rapidly, creating opportunities for fake plays and unexpected rotations.

Visually, Bind has a Middle Eastern desert theme, featuring sandstone buildings, narrow alleyways, and open courtyards. The balance between indoor and outdoor spaces forces teams to carefully manage utility and movement.
