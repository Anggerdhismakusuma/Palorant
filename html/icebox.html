MAP - ICEBOX

Icebox is a snow-covered, industrial-themed map set in a frozen tundra, featuring tight corridors, vertical elements, and multiple zip lines. The map is known for its complex site structures, requiring precise movement and well-coordinated team plays to navigate effectively.

Both A and B bomb sites offer high-ground positions, making vertical engagements crucial. Mid control is also essential, allowing for faster rotations and flanking opportunities. The presence of crates, tunnels, and ziplines creates a dynamic battleground where players must use both elevation and cover to outmaneuver their opponents.
