MAP - BREEZE

Breeze is a large, open map set on a tropical island, featuring wide sightlines, open spaces, and minimal choke points. Unlike many other maps in Valorant, Breeze offers a more long-range combat experience, making sniper rifles like the Operator highly effective.

The map consists of two bomb sites (A and B), both of which provide multiple entry points and require strong team coordination to control. A Site is characterized by its pyramids, offering unique cover opportunities, while B Site features a long corridor and high-ground advantage. Mid control is crucial, as it allows for quick rotations and unexpected flanks.

Breeze also stands out with its coastal theme, featuring sandy beaches, palm trees, and ancient ruins, creating a visually stunning battleground. The mix of wide areas and vertical elements challenges players to adapt their strategies, balancing aggressive peeks with well-planned defenses.
